The House of Bernadotte has reigned in Sweden, an ancient monarchy, since 1818. The image above pictures King Carl XVI Gustav, his wife, children and grandchildren, a lineage which included the first nine members in line for the throne.

With a thousand years of traditions to rest on, the Monarchy of Sweden is one of the strongest remaining monarchies in the world, having been in place since before recorded time. King Carl XVI Gustav is the current monarch.
